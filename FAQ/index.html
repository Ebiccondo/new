FAQ

1How to find condo games?

To find easily go to our main page, look for the section "Our Condo Games" or the other section "More Condo Games", Remember: To see the games you must verify yourself as a real person by checking "I'm not a robot".

2 How to create a condo game?

To create go to "Create" select the type of game you want to generate, you can customize the game, then press "Generate Now", Solve the captcha and the game will be ready in a few seconds!.

3 Are the games safe?

Yes, they are safe, we have security against spam, scams, sending personal information is not allowed, we have a different chat from roblox to protect messages, all games have privacy included and all players must respect the rules otherwise they will be banned of all our games.

4 Does the HeadAdmin rank work? Its safe to buy?

Yes, when you purchase the HeadAdmin rank, Paypal will verify the purchase, then your username will be added to our encrypted HeadAdmin list.
The game has access to that database, it will search for your username and if they match, the server will give you the HeadAdmin rank.

5 Why do I have errors?

You may have some bugs in the game, we are actively working to fix it, if you have a bug please contact us on Discord, maybe that bug will be fixed in a few hours.

6 If I generate a game, can my account be banned?

No, it is true that roblox can ban your main account if you create a condo game in another account, due to the IP address, cookies, etc. On our page when you generate a game your IP address is not involved, the IP of our servers be involved, your accounts are safe, if you do not trust us you can use a VPN.
